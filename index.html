Hello :)

I am a Ph.D. student at the Quantum-Safe Design and Analysis Lab (QSDAL) in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Roorkee, where I have been advised by Prof. Sugata Gangopadhyay since 2021.

My research focuses on the construction and analysis of cryptographic schemes based on lattice assumptions. In particular, I am interested in understanding how the underlying algebraic structures influence both the security assumptions and the practical security of lattice-based cryptographic schemes.

Before joining IIT Roorkee, I obtained my Master’s degree in Cyber Security from Jain University, India, and my Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from Latakia University (formerly Tishreen University), Syria.
