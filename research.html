Giant Does NOT Mean Strong: Cryptanalysis of BQTRU
A. Raya, V. Kumar, A. K. Gangopadhyay, and S. Gangopadhyay
Post-Quantum Cryptography – PQCrypto 2025, Springer, pp. 312–348
